About

Dr. Thitipong Panthum is a researcher specializing in animal genomics, cytogenetics, and wildlife forensics. His work focuses on genome organization, sex chromosome evolution, and biodiversity conservation in vertebrates. His research integrates comparative genomics, molecular cytogenetics, and bioinformatics to support sustainable aquaculture and wildlife conservation.
