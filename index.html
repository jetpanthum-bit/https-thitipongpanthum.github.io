About

Dr. Thitipong Panthum is a researcher in animal genomics and molecular cytogenetics. His research focuses on genome evolution, sex determination systems, and comparative genomics across aquatic and vertebrate species. His work integrates genome sequencing technologies, bioinformatics pipelines, and evolutionary biology to understand chromosome architecture and genetic diversity.
